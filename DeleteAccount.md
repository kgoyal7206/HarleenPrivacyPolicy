# Delete your Harleen account and data

This page explains how to request deletion of your **Harleen** account and associated data.

## How to request deletion

Email us from the email address you used to create your Harleen account.

- **Email:** krish@harleenapp.com  
- **Subject:** Delete my Harleen account  
- **Include in the email:**
  1. The email address you used in Harleen
  2. A clear statement that you want your account deleted

We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before we delete anything.

## What will be deleted

When your deletion request is approved, we will delete:

- Your Harleen account (email and profile)
- Your meal history entries
- Your meal photos you uploaded (if stored on our servers)
- App settings linked to your account (e.g., insulin-to-carb ratio, targets)

## What may be retained

We may retain limited information for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance:

- Basic security logs (e.g., timestamps, IP addresses) retained for up to **30 days**

## Timeframe

We aim to complete deletion within **7 days** after verifying the request.

## Contact

If you have questions, contact: **krish@harleenapp.com**
